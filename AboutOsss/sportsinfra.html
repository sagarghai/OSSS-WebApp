Sports forms an integral part of student life at Oxford Senior Secondary School. The school recognizes the importance of sports and physical activity in the overall development of its pupils, and strives to develop itself into a centre of excellence for sports related activities. Many of our students have utilized the platform and have entered centres of higher learning through sports quota. Excellent infrastructure, manned by highly qualified coaches and Physical Education teachers, results in our students participating in competitions upto National Level.
